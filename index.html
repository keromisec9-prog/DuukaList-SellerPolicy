Seller Policy

This Seller Policy outlines the rules, expectations, and responsibilities for users who publish listings or offer goods or services on DuukaList+.

By listing items or services on the platform, you agree to this Policy.

1. Independent Sellers

All sellers on DuukaList+ operate independently. DuukaList+ does not act as a broker, agent, retailer, reseller, or intermediary for sellers.

Sellers are solely responsible for their listings, communication, transactions, pricing, and fulfillment.

2. Accurate and Honest Listings

Sellers agree to ensure that their listings:

  • Are truthful, accurate, and not misleading.
  • Contain only content the seller has rights to publish.
  • Reflect the actual condition, price, and availability of the item or service.
  • Do not violate any laws or third-party intellectual property rights.

3. Prohibited Seller Conduct

Sellers may not:

  • List illegal, restricted, or harmful items or services.
  • Engage in fraud, misrepresentation, or deceptive practices.
  • Upload counterfeit or stolen items.
  • Use false identities or impersonate others.
  • Use automated tools to publish content without permission.
  • Post duplicate, spam, or excessively repeated listings.

4. Communication With Buyers

Sellers must communicate respectfully and clearly with buyers. Sellers are responsible for:

  • Answering buyer questions honestly.
  • Providing complete and accurate information.
  • Not harassing, threatening, or abusing buyers.

5. Independent Transactions

All transactions between buyers and sellers occur independently outside DuukaList+. DuukaList+:

  • Does not handle payments.
  • Does not guarantee delivery or quality.
  • Does not provide refunds on behalf of sellers.
  • Does not mediate disputes or enforce agreements.

6. Payment Responsibility

Payment processing is handled solely by third-party providers. DuukaList+ does not store, access, or manage card details or financial information.

Any payment error, dispute, or refund must be handled directly between the buyer, the seller, and the payment provider involved.

7. Seller Liability

Sellers are fully responsible for any issues arising from their listings or transactions, including:

  • Disputes with buyers.
  • Item quality, accuracy, and delivery.
  • Legal compliance.
  • Refunds or cancellations.

8. Platform Moderation

DuukaList+ may remove listings or restrict seller accounts that violate platform rules or pose risks to users, but is not obligated to do so.
